About the CSE Club of HSTU

Overview

The CSE Club of HSTU is a dynamic and inclusive community for students and faculty of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University. The club fosters innovation, collaboration, and personal growth through a variety of academic, technical, and social activities.

"Empowering minds,
Building futures,
Creating innovation"

Objectives and Vision

  • To promote interest and excellence in computer science and engineering among students.
  • To organize seminars, workshops, coding contests, and tech talks for skill development.
  • To encourage teamwork, leadership, and networking among members.
  • To bridge the gap between academia and industry through events and collaborations.
  • To inspire innovation and research in emerging technologies.

Our Mission

We strive to create a vibrant ecosystem where students can explore, learn, and grow together. Through collaborative projects, mentorship programs, and industry partnerships, we aim to prepare the next generation of technology leaders.

Brief History

The CSE Club of HSTU was founded in 2012 by a group of passionate students and faculty members. Since its inception, the club has achieved several milestones:

  • 2012: Club established and first executive committee formed.
  • 2014: Hosted the first inter-university programming contest at HSTU.
  • 2017: Launched annual tech seminar series and workshops.
  • 2020: Initiated online events and webinars during the pandemic.
  • 2023: Membership reached 500+ students and alumni.
